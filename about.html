About Us

Over the past few years, artists have been struggling to nail themselves an opporrtunity to be recognised in the music lime light. That made a lot of artists with huge potential to give up on their dreams. We are here to change all of that and simplify the break through into the music industry. That's who we are, our goal over the long-term is to create a competitive music industry.

Our mission is to link artist with various record labes and give accessibility to music industry. We intend to do this by providing artists with a platform that will make the accessibility of music demo submissions simple

About Vision

Our vision is to see a huge breakthrough of new talent in the next coming decade. We are inspired by a huge amount of underground-talent that has huge potential and we intend to ensure that we offer the best services at the lowest possible cost.
